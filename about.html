About This Website

This website was created to showcase the film heritage of Arkansas. Many well or lesser known movies have been filmed in Arkansas, and many successful actors call the Natural State home.

This website was developed using:

    HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

    The TMDB API for movie and actor data

    Encyclopedia of Arkansas for Arkansas-specific research

    Google Analytics to analyze visitor traffic

This project is intended for audiences 20 to 40 years old who are interested in the films, actors, culture, and Arkansas history.

All movie posters, actor images, and filmographies are sourced using the TMDB API.
