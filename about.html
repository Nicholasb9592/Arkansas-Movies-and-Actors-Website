Our Mission

This website celebrates Arkansas’s contributions to film and the talented actors from our state. Using data from TMDB and the Encyclopedia of Arkansas, we highlight productions filmed here and the people who brought them to life.

Technologies Used

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • TMDB API for live movie and actor data
  • Google Analytics for site tracking
  • GitHub Pages for hosting
